What score do I need to enter the class?
The Masterclass is designed for students currently scoring between a 630 and 750, who are aiming for a 750-800. If you're below a 630, you can still join, but you'll need to work extra hard to keep up.
Why is the PrepPros book not included? How much is it?
I don't get free access to the book--I buy watermarked copies for each student. With students joining the asynchronous class constantly, it's much easier to handle book purchases on the students' end.

You can get a physical copy for $35 or a digital copy for $29.75 using the code ERIC15 at checkout.

And yes, every single student who joins the Masterclass should buy the book!
Can I email you with questions?
Short questions and logistical questions? Yes, of course!

Longer questions about content? Book a 1-on-1 session and we'll be able to go much deeper about the material than in an email conversation.
How should I follow the material from the class?
Upon purchasing, you'll be sent a syllabus that tells you exactly which chapters in SAT Math from the Ground Up and the PrepPros book to complete alongside each recording.

I also spend around 15 minutes in the first session detailing how best to utilize both resources. This is arguably the most important part of the entire class!
When exactly do the recordings get posted?
It takes a few hours for each recording to be processed, so I try to upload them to the syllabus 6-24 hours after each session.

And as a reminder, each cohort is ongoing up until 1-2 weeks before its respective SAT.
Do you guarantee a 750+?
I do not offer score guarantees. Almost every test prep platform with score guarantees has caveats in the fineprint (i.e. "must complete all 500 hours of material").

Instead, I let the results of my students speak for themselves. Every Masterclass student who has put the requisite time and effort to get a 750 has achieved it. I don't want a score guarantee to relieve students of accountability or disincentivize perseverance.
Can I get a refund if I change my mind?
Since purchasing the class instantly sends you the syllabus and all recordings, I do not offer refunds.

If you're unsure about joining, please reach out! I'm happy to address any questions or concerns.
