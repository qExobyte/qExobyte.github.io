FAQ

What is the schedule for next cohort?
Tenatively, the start date will be March 22, and sessions will be Saturdays 12:00 - 2:00 pm ET, leading up to the May SAT.

However, I'm still gathering feedback from the current cohort to see if any adjustments would be beneficial.
What if I need to miss a session?
No worries! Every session will be recorded, so you'll never get behind. If you have questions from the recording, you're welcome to send them by email.
What score do I need to enter the class?
The Masterclass is designed for students currently scoring between a 630 and 750, who are aiming for a 750-800. If you're below a 630, you can still join, but you'll need to work extra hard to keep up.
How small is the class?
It depends on how many students sign up but I'm aiming for around 10-15 students.

That being said, since the sessions are recorded, expect a lot of absences. Last cohort, I capped the class at 10 students and some classes had as few as 4-6 students. So don't worry, it's far more interactive than your typical high school class.
Do I need to buy myself the PrepPros book?
Nope! I provide every student with a digital copy of the book. If you'd prefer a physical copy, you can purchase one for yourself off Amazon.
Do you guarantee a 750+?
I do not offer score guarantees. Almost every test prep platform with score guarantees has caveats in the fineprint (i.e. "must complete all 500 hours of material").

Instead, I let the results of my students speak for themselves. Every Masterclass student who has put the requisite time and effort to get a 750 has achieved it. I don't want a score guarantee to relieve students of accountability or disincentivize perseverance.
What's covered in the review session?
The review session is probably my favorite part of the class. We play a series of games testing every topic at a high level, as well as the hardest problems from past SATs. There will be prizes for the top students (last cohort it was $50 haha).
Can I get a refund if I change my mind?
If you realize after the first session and before the second session that the class is not fit for you, I'll refund 3/4 of the class's price. So, if the class is $1000, I'll refund $750.

If you get a refund, you'll no longer have access to any of the class's recordings or resources. So far, however, I have not had a single student out of 40 students request a refund. Hope to keep it that way!
What if YOU (Eric) need to miss a session?
I've never cancelled a class, but in the case of sickness, technical difficulties, or an emergency, I might need to.

If I need to cancel, I'll send out a "when2meet" to see which times everyone is free, and reschedule the session to the best time. In the worst case scenario, you'll need to watch the recording.
